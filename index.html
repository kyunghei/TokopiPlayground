TokopiPlayground is a doggy daycare that provides a wide variety of services, including grooming, training, and walks. The business serves roughly 2,000 dogs in the community and generates $1 million in revenue annually. From the estimated 2,000 dogs serviced, more than half are repeat guests that visit at least once a month, with an estimated 800 dogs visiting weekly. Due to the large amount of transactions from all the pets being serviced, a comprehensive database containing relevant information is needed to facilitate and maintain efficient operation. We will create a database application that manages customer information, visit details, and invoices. The application will help organize customer invoices and service sales.
